## Revenue-Tier Scaling

Service pricing should scale to the target company's size and complexity:

### Enterprise (Revenue > 1B)
- Multiply base pricing by 1.5x - 3x
- Multi-year managed service contracts preferred
- Expect larger scope (global operations, multiple business units)
- Typical total engagement: 500K - 2M+ per year

### Mid-Market (Revenue 100M - 1B)
- Use base pricing as listed above
- Project-based engagements transitioning to managed services
- Typical total engagement: 200K - 800K per year

### SMB (Revenue < 100M)
- Multiply base pricing by 0.5x - 0.8x
- Bundled service packages, shorter engagements
- Typical total engagement: 50K - 300K per year

## 3-Year Opportunity Calculation

Structure the revenue opportunity across three years:

### Year 1: Assessment + Quick Wins (40-50% of total)
- Initial assessments: pentest, cloud assessment, architecture review, OT assessment
- Quick-win implementations: email security, EDR deployment, backup hardening
- Compliance gap closure for imminent deadlines (NIS2, DORA)
- IR retainer establishment
- vCISO engagement (if no internal CISO)

### Year 2: Implementation + Expansion (30-35% of total)
- Major implementations: SIEM/SOAR, IAM/PAM, zero trust architecture
- Managed service transitions: SOC, MDR, managed SIEM
- Certification programs: ISO 27001, SOC 2
- Expanded training: awareness platform, tabletop exercises
- Follow-up assessments: red team, app security

### Year 3: Managed Services + Ongoing (20-25% of total)
- Ongoing managed services: SOC, MDR, managed ASM, managed vuln scanning
- Annual reassessments: pentest, cloud review, risk assessment
- Program maturity reviews and roadmap updates
- Expansion to new business units or geographies
- Advanced services: threat intel, threat hunting

## Account Classification

Based on the estimated 3-year revenue opportunity:

| Classification | 3-Year Revenue | Characteristics |
|---------------|---------------|-----------------|
| **STRATEGIC PARTNER** | > 2M | Enterprise account, multiple service lines, strategic partnership potential |
| **KEY ACCOUNT** | 500K - 2M | Mid-to-large account, 3+ service opportunities, strong pain drivers |
| **TARGET ACCOUNT** | < 500K | Focused engagement, 1-2 service lines, clear entry point |

Classification determines engagement intensity:
- **STRATEGIC PARTNER**: Dedicated account team, custom proposals, executive-level engagement
- **KEY ACCOUNT**: Senior consultant lead, multi-phase proposal, director-level engagement
- **TARGET ACCOUNT**: Consultant lead, targeted proposal, manager-level engagement
